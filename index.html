Team Schedule

10-person · 12-hour shifts · split-week rotation · Aug 2025 – Aug 2027

How the schedule works

Team splits into Front Half (Sun–Tue, alternating Wed) and Back Half (alternating Wed, Thu–Sat). 12-hour shifts: days 6am–6pm, nights 6pm–6am. Roles rotate weekly (D1 → D2 → D3 → N1 → N2) in a 5-week cycle. Your days stay fixed—only your role changes.

How to use this tool

Drag operators between halves to explore team compositions. Position determines starting role (slot 1=D1, slot 5=N2). Switch to Individual view for your full 52-week schedule, or Crew view to see daily coverage by week.

📅
Your days never change
Front half always owns Sun–Tue. Back half always owns Thu–Sat. Plan your life a year out without guessing.
⚖
Fair rotation, no favorites
Every operator cycles through every role — D1, D2, D3, nights — on the exact same schedule. Nobody is stuck on nights permanently.
📅
More days off than a 5-day week
You work 3 days one week, 4 the next — averaging 3.5 per week. That's more consecutive days off to actually recharge.
🤝
Consistent teammates
You work alongside the same half every shift. You know your crew, they know you — better communication, fewer handoff errors.
💵
Your paycheck stays the same
Same total hours as the existing schedule means your base pay and overtime don’t change. No surprises on payday — what you earn now is what you’ll earn later.
